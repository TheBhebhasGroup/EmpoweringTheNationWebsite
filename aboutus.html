About Us

Empowering the Nation, founded by Precious Radebe in 2018, provides skills training for domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg.
Our mission is to enhance employability and support individuals in starting their own businesses.

What We Do

We offer comprehensive training programs, including:
  • Six-Month Training Programmes
  • Six-Week Short Skills Training Programmes
    • Our focus is on practical skills that meet market demands.

    What We Provide

    We offer:
  • Career counseling
  • Networking opportunities
  • Support in business development
